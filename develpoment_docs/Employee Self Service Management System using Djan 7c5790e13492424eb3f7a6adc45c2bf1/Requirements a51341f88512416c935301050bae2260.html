Requirements

Functional requirements

  • 24/7 availability of HR-related information, including company policies, procedures, and updates
  • Ability to manage and request personal time-off (PTO) with automated approval processes
  • Capability to update personal information, such as contact details, emergency contacts, and bank accounts
  • Time tracking functionality with integrations to payroll systems
  • Access to employee-related data, including performance reviews, salary history, and job descriptions
  • Access to company handbooks, rules, and information in multiple languages, if necessary
  • Access to and management of benefits, such as health insurance, 401ks, and other retirement plans
  • User-friendly interface with quick search and navigation features
  • Integration with other HR tools, such as recruiting and onboarding software
  • Customizable notifications and alerts for upcoming deadlines, important events, and policy updates
  • Robust reporting and analytics capabilities to track HR data and metrics
  • Secure data storage and protection of sensitive employee information

Non-Functional Requirements

  • Fast and responsive performance to ensure a seamless user experience
  • High availability and reliability to minimize downtime and ensure 24/7 access to information
  • Scalability to support growing employee numbers and expanding business needs
  • Compliance with data privacy regulations and industry standards, such as GDPR and SOC 2
